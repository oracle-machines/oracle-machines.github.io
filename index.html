ML is reponsible for the rise of AI. It's impact is expected to be on par with Electricity. Despite the hype it's not on the verge of achieving consciousness, it's a mathematical tool. The current techniques give AI a narrow expertise, magical but brittle. Lighting existed before the light bulb but the cost was higher, you only used them when necessary. AI is doing that for predictions which are at the core everthing. Vision and speech recognition can be thought as prediction problems. Computer Chess wasn't the death of Chess. AI will not bring about our redundancy. AI will take over the mundane at scale leaving us to focus on the creative and human aspects of our lives but we will still need People to supervise, train and build AI.
